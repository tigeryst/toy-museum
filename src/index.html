Perhaps the largest and first of its kind in Asia, Batcat Museum and Toy owns the largest collection of superheroes' (Batman, Superman, Spiderman, to name a few) collectibles and toys, ranging from mini-figures to life-sized models. Imagine you will be surrounded by some 50,000 collectibles of superheroes you were dreaming of when you were just a kid. Some of which were created in the 60s and no longer on display elsewhere.

Alongside our large collection of toys, we also hold special exhibitions. You may check the current exhibitions by following the link above.
